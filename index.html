About me

I am a passionate full-stack developer with a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies. I hold a BSc Hons in Computing from Leeds Beckett University and have gained solid experience in ASP.Net, Laravel, and PHP. My commitment to continuous learning led me to move to Canada, where I pursued and completed a Postgraduate Certificate in Cybersecurity at Durham College. I became increasingly aware of how integral cybersecurity is to web development. In today’s digital landscape, the need to protect web applications from vulnerabilities, data breaches, and malicious attacks is paramount. This realization has driven me to integrate security best practices directly into my web development workflow. Whether I'm developing a front-end interface or working on back-end functionality, security remains a core focus. Currently, I am further expanding my expertise by pursuing a Postgraduate Certificate in Frontend Design and Development at George Brown College. Additionally, I continue to enhance my knowledge through online courses in MERN, further ensuring that the applications I build are secure, scalable, and resilient. Beyond my professional pursuits, I have a diverse range of hobbies that keep me engaged and inspired. I am a gamer, enjoying titles like Clash of Clans, Valorant, and GTA etc. In my leisure time, I also love to strum my guitar, watch a variety of movies, and explore new places through traveling and trekking. These activities not only provide me with relaxation but also fuel my creativity and problem-solving skills, which I bring back to my work as a developer.
